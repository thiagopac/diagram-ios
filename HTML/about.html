About Stockfish

Stockfish is a free multi-platform chess program. The source code can be downloaded from the Stockfish web site.

  • Version: Stockfish for iOS 2.13.0, 2014-10-26
  • Author e-mail: tord.romstad@gmail.com
  • Web site: http://www.stockfishchess.org
  • Copyright (C) Tord Romstad, Marco Costalba, Joona Kiiski 2009-2014

Stockfish's icon was generously contributed by Klein Maetschke. The "Modern" and "Russian" piece sets were designed by Bryan Whitby, and are used with his permission.
